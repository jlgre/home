Grilled brussles sprouts - that's it.

## Ingredients

-   **Brussels sprouts**: As many as I want to eat

## Directions

1. Wash and half brussels sprouts.
1. Put them on skewers.
1. Season and coat with oil.
1. Sear over high heat until deeply charred.
